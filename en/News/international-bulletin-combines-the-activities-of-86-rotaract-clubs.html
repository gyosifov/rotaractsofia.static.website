INTERNATIONAL BULLETIN COMBINES THE ACTIVITIES OF 86 ROTARACT CLUBS

5/14/2020

Rotary connects the world!

We are excited to introduce you to IJB - International Joint Bulletin initiated by Rotaract Club Islamabad, District 3282, Bangladesh with the participation of Rotaract Club Sofia. The newsletter brings together and presents 86 Rotaract clubs from 39 countries. Get to know their projects, culture and traditions!

Congratulations to the friends from Rotaract Club Islamabad for the great initiative!
