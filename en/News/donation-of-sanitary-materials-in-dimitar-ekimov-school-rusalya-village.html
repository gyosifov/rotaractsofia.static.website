DONATION OF SANITARY MATERIALS IN DIMITAR EKIMOV SCHOOL, RUSALYA VILLAGE

5/11/2020

There is nothing better than children's smiles!

In these difficult times, Rotaract Club Sofia once again gave a few smiles to the children from Dimitar Ekimov School of Arts and Crafts for Socially Vulnerable Children, Rusalya Village.

The much-needed sanitary materials and games were donated, with which the time under quarantine passes faster.

We look forward to our next meeting with them!
