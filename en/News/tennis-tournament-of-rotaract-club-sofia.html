TENNIS TOURNAMENT OF ROTARACT CLUB SOFIA

7/18/2020

July 18, 2020 (Saturday)

10.00 hours

Tennis courts of Tech Sport to Sofia Tech Park (111 Tsarigradsko shose Blvd.)

👉Registration - here

 

Welcome to the 26th annual Rotaract Club Sofia Tennis Tournament!

The tournament is for amateurs and is open to members of Interact, Rotaract and Rotary, as well as to outside guests. It will be held on 4 hard courts, and traditionally the competition is in two separate, parallel competitions: singles women and singles men.

We have also organized fun activities with a tennis coach for those who will not compete or just want to have fun.

The cause of the event is the construction of a playground in the Special School for Visually Impaired Students 'Louis Braille', Sofia.

Price for participation:

Interact: BGN 10

Rotaract / Guests: BGN 25

Rotary: BGN 30

The seats are limited. Save your place by registering here.

The tournament is held with the support of Tech Sport - Tennis Club and sports facilities at Sofia Tech Park.

We are expecting you!
