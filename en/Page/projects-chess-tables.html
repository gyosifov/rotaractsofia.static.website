CHESS TABLES

Rotaract Club Sofia and Rotary Club Sofia placed three chess tables in Sofia City Garden in 2015. The young talents from Chess Club “Elite” were among the first to play a demonstration game at the new tables.

The project received great response! Soon after, Rotaract Club Sofia together with Rotary Club Sofia-City placed three more chess tables in the garden in front of the Cultural Center “Krasno Selo”.

We are happy to see chess fans sitting down every day to play another game!

Thanks to Stefan Valev for making the metal frames and to Gama M&G for the great granite countertops!
